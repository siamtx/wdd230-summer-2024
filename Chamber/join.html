Membership Level Descriptions

Non-Profit Membership

Cost: Free

Benefits:

  • Access to special events for non-profit organization
  • Limited training sessions
  • Basic advertising options
  • Event discouts (up to 10%)

Bronze Membership

Cost: $50/year

Benefits:

  • All Non-Profit benefits
  • Additional training sessions
  • Enhanced advertising options (including spotlight positions on secondary pages)
  • Event discouts (up to 15%)
  • Access to member-only webinars

Silver Membership

Cost: $100/year

Benefits:

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Priority listing in directory
  • Exclusive spotlight positions on the home page
  • Higher event discounts (up to 20%)
  • Invitations to exclusive networking events

Gold Membership

Cost: $200/year

Benefits:

  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium advertising options (including top banner positions)
  • Maximum event discounts (up to 25%)
  • Free access to premium training and workshops
  • VIP access to all events

Platinum Membership

Cost: $500/year

Benefits:

  • All Gold benefits
  • Elite advertising options (including homepage banner and newsletter features)
  • Highest event discounts (up to 30%)
  • Personal business consultation services
  • Free entry to all chamber events
  • Exclusive inviatation to high-profile networking events
  • Recognition as a Platinum member on all chamber materials
  • Priority support and services

Diamond Membership

Cost: $1000/year

Benefits:

  • All Platnium benefits
  • Exclusive access to high-profile events with industry leaders
  • Personal business growth strategy sessions
  • Priority placement in chamber publications and marketing materials
  • Complimentary tickets to all chamber events
  • VIP concierge service to all chamber-related inquiries
  • Invitation to join the Chamber's advisory board
