White Space and Clean Design

Classic Furniture

Diana Danieli brought home a Webby award in 2019 due in large part to its sleek architecture, high-contrast imagery, and engaging layout. Visitors can browse stunning photos, open menus, and learn more about the successful artist who manages the website. The beautiful artistic pieces highlighted on the site can inspire, captivate, and intrigue on their own. Even so, website designers wisely chose to elevate these eye-catching pieces of visual content with music and other audio overlays.

Rule of Thirds

Octopus Incubator

An invitation only website providing studio, idea lab and incubator. purple > the production [arm] of octopus with decades of experience in multiple disciplines of art + technology + entertainment + gaming + hospitality. Idea lab is functioning like a mimic octopus changing forms + direction in a stealth capacity infusing disruptive ideas & innovations to change the landscape of society with technology + entertainment + gaming + hospitality. Incubator is the octopus [garden] = seed capitol + cultivating ideas in technology + entertainment + gaming.

Visual Hierarchy

Curious & Company

Those curious enough to navigate past the main page are greeted by an all-seeing eye, which maneuvers about the screen in an elliptical pattern. Below the eye is the site’s menu, which features Service, About, and Contact tabs. The site does a great job of drawing users’ attention to the right elements while keeping them engaged and preventing their minds from wandering.
