Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

  • Classic Furniture

  • Diana Danieli brought home a Webby award in 2019 due in large part to its sleek architecture, high-contrast imagery, and engaging layout. Visitors can browse stunning photos, open menus, and learn more about the successful artist who manages the website.

Rule of Thirds

  • Octopus Incubator

  • This website is colorful and divided into three parts. Uniquely, the 'Rule of Thirds' is both visual and technical.

Visual Hierarchy

  • Curious & Company

  • Those curious enough to navigate past the main page are greeted by an all-seeing eye, which maneuvers about the screen in an elliptical pattern. Below the eye is the site’s menu, which features Service, About, and Contact tabs. The site does a great job of drawing users’ attention to the right elements while keeping them engaged and preventing their minds from wandering.
